FMCG/Retail digital technology adoption rates (2019-2025)

Source: McKinsey ConsumerWise Sentiment Survey, Deloitte Retail Industry Outlook 2025

Pandemic Acceleration
COVID-19 compressed 5-10 years of digital adoption into months, with e-commerce penetration jumping from 16% to 35% in 2020 alone.
Mobile Commerce Growth
Mobile shopping now accounts for 67% of all digital retail transactions, up from 43% in 2019, with in-app purchases growing at 28% annually.
AI-Powered Personalization
70% of retailers now use AI for personalization, with early adopters reporting 15% higher conversion rates and 23% increase in average order value.
