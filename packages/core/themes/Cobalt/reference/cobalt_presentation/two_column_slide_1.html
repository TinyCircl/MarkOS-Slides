Traditional vs. Cobalt Design

A comparative analysis of presentation frameworks

VS

Traditional Design

Cluttered layouts with excessive information density
Inconsistent spacing and alignment across slides
Overused stock photos that lack contextual relevance
Generic color schemes without strategic hierarchy

Cobalt Design

Structured grid system for optimal content flow
Precise 40px margins and 24px internal spacing
Data-focused visuals and meaningful infographics
Strategic blue palette (#1f4788) for visual authority
